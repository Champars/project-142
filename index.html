How To Play AI Ping Pong

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move 3-4 feet away
  3. Move your right wrist in ascending and descending order a red dot should appear
  4. Now press play and move 3-4 feet away
  5. The red paddel is yours and will move as per the movement of your right wrist
